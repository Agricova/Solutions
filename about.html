AGRICOVA STORY

AGRICOVA was developed to meet the needs of commercial agriculture and horticulture cultivation. The name itself is a union of the words AGRICULTURE + NOVA. AGRICULTURE represents “the science or practice of farming” and “the cultivation of soil for the growing of crops,” while NOVA means “a star showing a sudden large increase in brightness.”

The logo is composed of three leaves in the shape of a flower, representing our commitment to agriculture and floral industries. The three leaves also symbolize our three core product lines.

Proudly delivering quality since 2010, AGRICOVA has grown alongside the evolving needs of commercial cultivators across North America.

AGRICOVA proudly distributes world‑class brands such as AUVL, PHILIPS, VOSTERMANS Ventilation, and more. With over ten years of intensive research and development, we ensure top‑tier products and service that translate into our customers’ success.
